About Us


Thai Signatures is an authentic Thai Restaurant that will surely take your senses back to the land of smiles. At Thai Signatures we believe the key elements of authentic Thai cuisine are the authentic Siamese spices and ingredients. All our ingredients are imported premium products. We can assure you that you won't be disappointed. Our headchef, Miss Rarin and her very talented team enjoys catering to the customers and produce food that is fresh and hygenic and cooked to perfection on a daily basis.We have diligently selected our menu to establish the perfect balance of lunch and dinner with a variety of pleasant and delicious dishes. Thai Signatures is upscale, but never uptight, with a welcoming neighborhood vibe and outstanding service.
